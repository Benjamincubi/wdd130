History

Raftventure: From the Open Road to the Canyon

The story of Raftventure didn't begin in an office; it began with an old map and an unquenchable thirst for adventure. Founded by two brothers, this project was born from a shared passion: seeking out and conquering the unknown.

Since we were young, the car was our vehicle to the extreme. Every weekend, my brother and I embarked on the same mission: to drive off the conventional map, using adrenaline as fuel and family trust as our only insurance. The destination didn't matter; the challenge did.

It was during one of these relentless searches, after countless miles and local rumors, that we discovered this unparalleled canyon and its untamed waters. What we found was more than a Class V river; it was a natural treasure and, we realized, the perfect location to build something unique.

We decided that the thrill we felt descending those rapids deserved to be shared, but only if we could guarantee the absolute safety that every extreme environment demands. Thus, we established Raftventure as a family business where rigorous planning, elite equipment, and a passion for the river converge.

Today, that spirit of exploration is still very much alive. Our establishment is not just a base of operations; it is the testament to a family dream: to offer the most extreme adventure under the most unbreakable code of safety.
